---
title: "What is Vedana"
section: "Concepts"
---

**Vedana is a system that makes AI outputs reliable and verifiable by grounding them in structured knowledge.**

Vedana combines vector search, structured data retrieval, and multi-step reasoning over data to produce answers that are traceable, reproducible, and aligned with real business logic.

Unlike typical RAG solutions, Vedana does not rely on raw vector search or single-pass generation.
It enables AI systems to explore data step-by-step, following an explicit data model and controlled reasoning process.

This allows companies to:

- enforce how answers are constructed
- verify every step of reasoning
- and reduce hallucinations.

Vedana is designed for domains where correctness matters - such as legal, e-commerce, and internal knowledge systems.
